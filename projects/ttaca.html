1. Identify Opportunity

In 2024, I joined TikTok's performance ad design team to lead design for web & app commerce vertical. When I first joined, design was only involved after product team decided on ideas. To get invoIved earlier and contribute to the ideation process, I created a user journey map and led a in-person workshop to identify top opportunities, and brainstorm solutions together with XFNs.

From the workshop, we identified ‘creative’ as a top opportunity with high business and user value. And I started leading a vision design project with my stakeholders to guide our product roadmap.

6. Launch & Results

The features were planned in 6 phases across 3 years due to the foundational impact of these designs and the complexity of the ad platform. We carefully conducted A/B testings for each phase, and used the data to iterate the design and maximize results. By mid-2024, we launched 3 phases with 30+ big and small projects across all business lines. During this process, I also mentored a junior designer to help share the workload and free up bandwidth for other key initiatives.
